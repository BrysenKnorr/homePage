What is FBLA?

Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA) is a national co-curricular organization for high school students taking business classes. Members learn about careers, build leadership skills, attend conferences, and compete in business and information technology events. This is a visible organization well known to business leaders around the country. FBLA gives students a head start in the business world and is a meaningful addition to his/her resume.


FBLA helps high school students prepare for careers in business through academic competitions, leadership development, and educational programs. The mission of FBLA is to “inspire and prepare students to become community-minded business leaders in a global society through relevant career preparation and leadership experiences.” With a mission to help students become community-minded business leaders, FBLA built its programs to reach that goal. Overall, FBLA has six specific areas focused on helping students become successful business leaders: Leadership development, Academic competitions, Educational programs, Membership benefits, Community service, Awards & recognition
